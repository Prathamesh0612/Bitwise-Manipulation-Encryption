About BME

BME (BitWise Manipulation Encryption) represents a new frontier in file security, merging advanced, partition-based encryption with the predictive power of artificial intelligence. Our system is designed from the ground up to be both incredibly secure and remarkably efficient.

Unlike traditional encryption methods that create a uniform block of cipher-text, BME's AI engine analyzes each file's unique data structure. It then intelligently partitions the file and applies a custom-weighted encryption strategy, creating an ultra-compact key that is up to 95% smaller than the raw data it secures.

Our Mission

To provide military-grade, AI-enhanced encryption that is accessible, efficient, and resilient against future threats. We believe security should not be a bottleneck, but an intelligent, integrated part of your workflow.

Our Technology

At the heart of BME is a multi-output TensorFlow model. It analyzes file entropy, structure, and potential corruption ratios to generate a unique "encryption fingerprint," optimizing both security and key compression.

Our Vision

We envision a future where data security is predictive, not just reactive. By leveraging AI, BME is the first step towards a system that not only encrypts data but also understands it, providing unparalleled protection.
